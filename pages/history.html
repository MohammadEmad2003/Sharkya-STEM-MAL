History

Model Arab League established on 11 June 2021 by group of students in Sharkya STEM School who believed that there is something that should be added to our community
We started to build a great team that each one learnt new thing beside the skills of his committee which is debating skills. Finally, we made our conference on 4 November 2021 which discussed the problem of Nahda Dam between Egypt, Sudan, and Ethiopia with the presence of Ethiopian delegates, and they reached a great results.
